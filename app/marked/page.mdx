# Paper: Enhancing Peer Feedback in MarkEd

*After a semester spent marking second-year students&apos; essays, I proposed a dissertation project aimed at improving assessment practices in education.*

Under the guidance of my amazing supervisors <Avatar person={Person.CristinaAlexandru} /> and <Avatar person={Person.AuroraConstantin} />, we extended MarkEd to address common issues in peer assessment. It combines structured moderation from human markers with AI-generated guidance to help students write more effective and reflective peer feedback.

The system was trialled in a large second-year course at the University of Edinburgh with 194 students. Of the students who received AI suggestions, 75% agreed or strongly agreed that they prompted reflection. The dissertation itself received a grade of *Outstanding (93%)*.
