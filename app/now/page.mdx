## Still working on my LED screen

It started as a fun object for myself, but I am now wondering whether I could actually sell a few of them through this website. Maybe just made to order. Maybe just a small batch. Not sure yet.

The exciting part is that it would be my first physical product that people might actually want.

The hard part is... basically everything else.

- It is over 70cm long, thin, and fragile. How do I ship that without it arriving snapped in half?
- If something breaks, what happens? Replace it? Do they ship it back to me?
- Fires? Shorts? Heat dissipation?
- Could I support over-the-air updates?

At the moment I am designing a 3D printed case for the back of the screen and adding some I/O. I want the cables hidden, the mounting to feel solid, and the whole thing to look less like a prototype and more like a product.

If you might want one or you have experience that could be helpful, please get in touch!
